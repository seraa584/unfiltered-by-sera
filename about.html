About Sera Anna Aby & The Unfiltered Project

Hi! My name is Sera Anna Aby, and I'm a high school student with a deep passion for neuroscience and its application to social issues. My goal is to educate the public on forgotten issues by exploring the complex patterns of human behavior and societal pressures that affect the brain and mind.

My Mission

The mission of UnfilteredBySera is to peel back the layers on hidden and overlooked social issues and analyze them through a unique lens, combining rigorous reporting with principles of psychology, neurobiology, and data analysis.

This space is dedicated to using analytical writing—like my work on Medium—to advocate for informed, empathetic solutions. By breaking down complex social dynamics into observable patterns and neural responses, I hope to offer perspectives that encourage deeper understanding and meaningful action.
